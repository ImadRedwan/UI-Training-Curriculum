# Code of Conduct

All participants, mentors, and contributors in the **UI Training Curriculum** are expected to adhere to the following Code of Conduct. Our goal is to create a **professional, respectful, and inclusive learning environment**.

---

## 1. Be Respectful

- Treat everyone with courtesy and professionalism.
- Respect different opinions, backgrounds, and levels of experience.
- Provide constructive feedback without being dismissive or offensive.

---

## 2. Collaboration

- Work together and support each otherâ€™s learning journey.
- Share knowledge generously and be open to learning from others.
- Avoid disruptive behavior that hinders collaboration.

---

## 3. Communication

- Use clear, professional, and polite language in all communication (PR comments, chat, meetings).
- Ask questions when unsure, and provide explanations when giving feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on learning and problem-solving.

---

## 4. Inclusivity

- Everyone is welcome regardless of age or background.
- Avoid language or behavior that may exclude, intimidate, or offend others.
- Encourage diverse perspectives and value contributions from all participants.

---

## 5. Accountability

- Take responsibility for your work, commits, and contributions.
- Follow through on commitments and respect deadlines.
- Admit mistakes, learn from them, and help maintain a positive learning environment.

---

## 6. Conflict Resolution

- If conflicts arise, address them calmly and professionally.
- Seek guidance from a mentor if needed.
- Focus on solutions rather than assigning blame.

---

By participating in the **UI Training Curriculum**, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct and help maintain a **positive, respectful, and productive environment** for everyone.
